AristanderAI

Roles and responsibilities

  • Role: Product Designer,
  • Services: User Interface & User Expierience design,
  • Team: Reimar Servas - Art Director and Ingrid Jacquet - Frontend Developer,
  • Company: Yoshino.digital, Berlin.

AristanderAI is a Berlin-based startup aspiring to revolutionise pricing sector. It uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to predict and adjust prices accordingly to customers willingness-to-pay.

Based on automated analysis how people interact with an e-commerce website (when they buy and when they don't buy), AristanderAI measures how willing they are to finalise the transaction and then calculates the perfect price.

The challenge

We teamed up with AristanderAI to build a strong digital strategy that would allow them to be recognisable and competitive in the market. Like any other company that starts up, they needed a digital business card that provides crucial information, serves as a contact point and helps acquire new partners.

Discovery

Working alongside the AristanderAI team allowed me to understand the product's perspectives, established requirements and priorities. I created the information architecture and interaction model, which provide a clear picture of what the website needed to contain.

Due to time constraints and the project's size, it was impossible to launch the whole website at once. Therefore, we decided to focus on bite-sized steps and 2-week iterations. Individual parts were planned in sprints that eventually add up to the original goal.

Audience

AristanderAI offers B2B product targeted to any size e-commerce businesses. The persona I created for the website is the decision-maker and internal influencer who impact the company's decisions.
