Profile

Long story short

  • Karo Karolina Wieczorek,
  • Designer - developer hybrid,
  • Senior Product Designer at Mimi Hearing,
  • 9-year experience in Design & UX sector,
  • Focused on design systems and accessibility,
  • BASc in Computer Science,
  • Based in Berlin, Germany.

My real value comes from 9-year hands-on work experience in Design & UX sector, topped with a traditional computer science degree. Alongside, empathy and being curious about the world around me are my minor superpowers that drive my work. I put people before pixels and always seek to create products that respect humans. I'm an aesthete at heart with a special treat for typography and minimalism. Combining all above allows me to design human-friendly products that meet business goals and provide everyone with equal opportunities. However, my primary expertise lies in design, I also enjoy tackling with code.

I'm known for my strong systems thinking mindset and sheer can-do attitude. I manage and lead design projects from an idea to pixel-perfect, implementation ready specifications. I efficiently and effectively communicate my concepts, articulating how they solve the problem at hand. I'm an empathetic user advocate who uses data to inform design decisions.

Services

I don't claim I design everything, but I'm skilled to help with projects which scope demands include:

  • Product Design

    I'm an integral part of the team throughout the entire design process: from a rough strategy, research and ideation, to defining the creative direction and delivering meaningful user experiences to overseeing the implementation. I make sure the visuals, interactions, and brand are all at their best and benefit both the user and the business.

  • Design Systems

    Developing visual languages and patterns that are more than a pile of reusable UI elements. My work's outcome is structured components library and documentation guide on why and how to use them.

  • Accessibility / Inclusive Design

    Performing accessibility audits and evaluations for websites and applications under consideration for their alignment with WCAG 2.0 accessibility standards.

  • User Experience

    Developing research / data-driven journey maps, user flows, and information architectures to validate assumptions, discover edge cases and deliver seamless interaction to an end-user.

  • User Interfac

    Translating concepts into visually attractive, functional and accessible interfaces while keeping technical / resources limitations in mind.

  • User Research / Usability Testing

    Discovering users insights / behaviours and addressing their needs in the creation process. I'm involved in qualitative research and later testing its results by running quantitative measures.

  • Rapid Ideation / Prototyping

    Creating high-fidelity, interactive prototypes for usability testing and documentation purposes.

  • Frontend Development

    Following the rule of progressive enhancement, I write semantic HTML and upgrade it with CSS (preferably Sass). When needed, I top it up with a layer of vanilla JavaScript. I am familiar with version control (GitHub / GitLab) and static site generators (11ty).
