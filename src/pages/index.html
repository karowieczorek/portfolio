Who I am

I'm Karo - an experienced product designer-developer hybrid with a key focus on design systems and accessibility. I spent the past 9 years helping various companies innovate and broadcast their full potential through design. As a Senior Product Designer at Mimi Hearing, I create a world where hearing impairment is no longer a barrier. Based in echt toll Berlin.

What I do

I solve problems with the design. My goal is to bring value and benefits for both: the user and the business. I'm a caretaker of the entire design project throughout its whole life cycle: from identifying, investigating and validating the problem, to prototyping and testing onto overseeing the implementation.
