Profile

Long story short

  • Karo Karolina Wieczorek,
  • Designer - developer hybrid,
  • Senior Product Designer at Mimi Hearing,
  • 9-year experience in Design & UX sector,
  • Focused on design systems and accessibility,
  • BASc in Computer Science,
  • Based in Berlin, Germany.

My real value comes from a reliable, hands-on work experience in Design & UX sector, topped with a traditional computer science degree. Alongside empathy and being curious about the world around me are my minor superpowers that drive my work. I put people before pixels and always seek to create products that respect humans. I'm an aesthete at heart with a special treat for typography and minimalism. Combining all above allows me to design human-friendly products that meet business goals and provide everyone with equal opportunities. However, my primary expertise lies in design, I also enjoy tackling with code.

I'm known for my strong systems thinking mindset and sheer can-do attitude. I manage and lead design projects from an idea to pixel-perfect, implementation ready specifications. I efficiently and effectively communicate my concepts, articulating how they solve the problem at hand. I'm an empathetic user advocate who values data to inform design decisions.
